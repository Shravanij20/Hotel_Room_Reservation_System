## Quick Deployment Steps:

1. **Go to Vercel**
   - Open: https://vercel.com
   - Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Log In"**

2. **Sign In with GitHub** (Easiest Method)
   - Click **"Continue with GitHub"**
   - Authorize Vercel to access your GitHub account
   - This allows automatic deployments from your repos

3. **Import Your Project**
   - After logging in, you'll see the Vercel dashboard
   - Click **"Add New..."** ‚Üí **"Project"**
   - You'll see a list of your GitHub repositories
   - Find and click: **"Hotel_Room_Reservation_System"**
   - Click **"Import"**

4. **Configure Project** (Usually auto-detected!)
   - **Framework Preset**: Should show "Vite" (auto-detected)
   - **Root Directory**: `./` (default - leave as is)
   - **Build Command**: `npm run build` (default - leave as is)
   - **Output Directory**: `dist` (default - leave as is)
   - **Install Command**: `npm install` (default - leave as is)
   
   ‚ö†Ô∏è **Usually you don't need to change anything!** Just verify these settings.

5. **Deploy!**
   - Click the big **"Deploy"** button
   - Wait 1-2 minutes for the build to complete
   - Watch the build logs (it will show progress)

6. **Get Your Live URL!**
   - After deployment completes, you'll see:
     - ‚úÖ Success message
